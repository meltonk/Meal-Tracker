Meal Tracker










The MealTracker application is an integrated tool to provide comprehensive meal tracking capabilities. Meal Tracker module keeps track of food program information needed for childcare providers for the USDA Child and Adult Care Food Program (CACEF) and also provides enhanced features for the detailed tracking of nutrition for individual food items served. The MealTracker application also tracks family eligibility for free and reduced lunch which helps generate monthly re-imbursements reports.


Meal Tracker Features

Count meals served for breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks. Record daycare meal counts based on child schedule attendance and count meals based on actural attendance.

  • Save Childs Information
  • Record Daily Meal Counts
  • Record Daily Meals Served
  • Keep track of daily Attendance

Food Program Reports

Get meal count totals for each status level like free, reduced, and paid. See meals claimed for reimbursement separate from noneligible meals. Print classroom meal rosters, menus, and meal count reports based on age group.

  • Weekly & Monthly Menus
  • Meal Roll Call Sheets
  • Age Group Meal Counts
  • Detailed Record of Meals Served & Claimed
  • Monthly Reibursement Calculation

Daycare Time & Attendance Tracking

With time and attendance tracking, you'll have child attendance and time sheet information available when need it.
